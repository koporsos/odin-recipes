Turkey Chili

This turkey chili recipe is very simple but hearty with plenty of flavors â€” it tastes even better on the second day! I serve this chili with low-fat Cheddar cheese and low-fat sour cream. It's also delicious with crackers.

ingredients

  • turkey
  • onion
  • garlic
  • canned vegetables
  • spices

Steps

  1. Brown the turkey in olive oil. Add the onion and cook until tender.
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes.
